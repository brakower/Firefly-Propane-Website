Meet Barrie

Firefly Propane was founded with a simple mission: to keep families safe, gatherings joyful, and outdoor living effortless. Our story begins with Barrie, a local Westchester native who spent her childhood grilling with her family every Sunday — rain or shine.

Those backyard meals weren't just about the food; they were about connection, laughter, and creating a space where people felt at home. Years later, after seeing too many neighbors struggle with malfunctioning grills and dangerous gas setups, Barrie realized there was a real need — not just for propane services, but for a company people could trust like family. That's when Firefly was born.

What began as a one-woman operation has grown into a trusted local business serving Westchester and Lower Fairfield Counties with integrity, transparency, and most importantly, an infectious smile. At Firefly, we're not just about propane — we're about people.
