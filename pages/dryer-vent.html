Warning Signs

Is it time to clean your dryer vent?

Clothes Take Longer to Dry

If a single cycle isn't enough, your vent is probably restricted. Blocked airflow means poor drying performance.

Dryer Gets Very Hot

An unusually hot dryer or clothes that feel scorching at the end of a cycle signals a serious ventilation issue.

Burning Smell

A burning odor when your dryer runs is a red flag. Lint is highly flammable — don't ignore this warning.

Vent Flap Doesn't Open

If the exterior vent flap doesn't open when the dryer runs, your vent is blocked and needs immediate attention.
