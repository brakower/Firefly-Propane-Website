The Science

How the Mosquito Magnet works

It's not magic — it's propane-powered science that mimics human breath to lure and trap mosquitoes.

Propane Converted

The trap converts propane into CO₂, mimicking human exhalation that attracts mosquitoes.

Mosquitoes Attracted

The combination of CO₂, heat, and moisture draws mosquitoes in from up to an acre away.

Trapped & Eliminated

A powerful vacuum net captures mosquitoes where they dehydrate — no chemicals needed.

Population Drops

Over 7–10 days, the local mosquito population is significantly reduced. Results build season over season.
