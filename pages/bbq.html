BBQ Clean & Repair

We clean, repair, and maintain grills across the area to keep your cookouts safe and delicious.

Step One

BBQ Cleaning

Our BBQ cleaning service ensures your grill is spotless and ready for your next cookout. We remove grease, ash, and food residue to keep your grill in top condition.

Why Clean Your Grill?

Regular grill cleaning prevents dangerous grease buildup, extends the life of your equipment, and ensures that every meal tastes great and cooks evenly. A clean grill is a safe grill — protect your family and enjoy your food without flare-ups or foul odors.

Deep clean of grates, burners, and drip trays
Grease and ash removal throughout
Inspect for corrosion or wear
Season grates for optimal cooking

Step Two

BBQ Repair

We offer comprehensive BBQ repair services to fix any issues with your grill. Whether it's a broken igniter, damaged burners, or any other problem, our technicians can help.

Why Repair Instead of Replace?

Grill repairs restore performance, prevent further damage, and save you money compared to buying new. Whether it's a faulty igniter, uneven heat, or damaged burners, fixing your grill keeps your cookouts consistent, safe, and stress-free.

Common Questions

BBQ Service FAQs

We recommend a deep clean at least once or twice a season — typically at the start of grilling season and again mid-summer if you grill often. Heavy use (multiple times a week) may call for quarterly cleanings to prevent grease buildup and flare-ups.

We work on propane, natural gas, and charcoal grills of nearly every make and model — from basic backyard grills to high-end built-in outdoor kitchen units.

Most standard cleanings take about 45–60 minutes. Repairs vary depending on the issue and parts availability, but we'll always give you a clear time estimate before we start.

We carry common replacement parts (igniters, burner tubes, hoses) on our trucks. For less common or model-specific parts, we'll source them and schedule a quick follow-up visit.

Of course! Many customers like to watch and ask questions — we're happy to walk you through what we're doing and share maintenance tips for keeping your grill in great shape between visits.
